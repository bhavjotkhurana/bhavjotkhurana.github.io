I’m a **data enthusiast with a background in statistics, machine learning, and education**, focused on using product data to understand how people learn, engage, and make decisions.

My work spans:
- **Predictive modeling**
- **Experimentation and causal inference**
- **Data infrastructure**  
  (everything needed to move from raw events to meaningful change in how we build tools and experiences)

For the past **15 months**, I’ve also worked as a **math and stats tutor**, supporting students across:
- Varying academic levels (high school, undergraduate, etc.)
- A range of learning styles and needs

This hands-on teaching experience has deepened my interest in the intersection of **education and data**:
- How can we design systems that adapt to learners—not the other way around?
- How can we use feedback loops and metrics to improve both engagement and outcomes?

### Projects and Current Interests

- **Rubric-based models** for tracking student performance and instructional impact  
- **Behavioral simulations** to better understand how students interact with learning tools  
- **qScroll** — a vertically-scrolling SAT-style test-prep app focused on:
  - Instant feedback
  - Minimalist design
  - A data-first approach to identifying and addressing learning gaps

I'm also exploring how to integrate **large language models (LLMs)** into real educational workflows, including:
- Intelligent tutoring interfaces
- Dataset generation and enrichment
- Adaptive feedback systems
